Ribsden Holt is approached via wrought iron entrance gates which open onto an exceptional driveway that winds gently up through the grounds. Views like these in this area are almost impossible to find. As you enter through an impressive front door the charm and character of Ribsden Holt is immediately apparent.

It also opens into the dining room so, combined, these rooms flow well for those who enjoy entertaining.
